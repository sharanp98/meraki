About Meraki

A melangè of opportunities

Meraki 2.0 celebrates the triumphant spirit of leadership, by honouring the women trailblazers who have left an indelible mark in their chosen field of career. It is an ode to the successful women who have changed the way we perceive the world - be it through art, technology or visionary innovation. A one-day event hosting speakers from diverse backgrounds, workshops and competitions, Meraki aims to inspire and initiate thoughtful action.

Meraki is conducted by the Women’s Cell of Sree Chitra Thirunal College of Engineering, Trivandrum under the aegis of Cult-A-Way 2019. The event is powered by Anita B.Org India.
